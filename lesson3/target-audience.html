Pioche Chamber of Commerce Target Audience

Site Purpose
Target Audience
Site Design

Description:

The typical demographic of Pioche is older aged people working at the small shops in town or retired. There is a small rise in younger to mid-aged families moving into town, but it is still a small part of the population.


Personas:

1. George - 65 years old, retired as an alfalfa farmer and now works at the town gas station as a cashier. Does not consider himself to be very knowledgable with technology, but is able to find his way around. He typically connects to the internet on his outdated laptop via satellite connection.

2. Betty - 70 years old, works as an antique shopkeeper. Sells home-made crochet and knitted goods. She has grown used to using the internet, Uses her phone or home desktop to access the internet mainly to order supplies or check social media for town events.


Scenarios:

1. Betty wants to arrange an arts and crafts event with some of the other businesses in town to hopefully bring in more people to the town. He needs to make sure there are no other events happening the same day he is planning on holding this event.

2. George has some work that needs to be done in the yard, but it would be too much for his back to do it himself. He needs a place to look for a local yard care business or a number to call to request community service.

3. George volunteered to be on the committee for the anual Labor Day celebrations. He needs a place to post the year's theme and activities once a schedule has been made.

4. Betty has a lot of inventory that she needs to get rid of to make room for renovations in the store. She needs a place to be able to post her clearnace sale dates and times.

5. George needs to bring a public safety issue about possibly installing a traffic light on Main Street, but the town council members have recently been changed. He needs to be able to look up the new council members and their contact information.
