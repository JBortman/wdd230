Description:

The typical demographic of Pioche is older aged people working at the small shops in town or retired. There is a small rise in younger to mid-aged families moving into town, but it is still a small part of the population.


Personas:

1. George - 65 years old, retired as an alfalfa farmer and now works at the town gas station as a cashier. Does not consider himself to be very knowledgable with technology, but is able to find his way around. He typically connects to the internet on his outdated laptop via satellite connection.

2. Betty - 70 years old, works as an antique shopkeeper. Sells home-made crochet and knitted goods. She has grown used to using the internet, Uses her phone or home desktop to access the internet mainly to order supplies or check social media for town events.


Scenarios:

1. Jeff, one of the town bar tenders, wants to arrange an event with the other bars in town to hopefully bring in more people to the town. He needs to make sure there are no other events happening the same day he is planning on holding this event.

2. Cassie and her family just finished building a house in the newer side of town and wants to see the businesses in town and when they are open, hopefully to grab some groceries for a quick dinner after unboxing.

3. Steven is planning a trip to Pioche for the annual Labor Day celebrations. He wants to check if the schedule of events has been posted yet and what the theme of the weekend will be.

4. Jannett just opened a business and wants to get her branding out around town. She needs a place to be able to post and share her products and hours.

5. Tom needs to bring a public safety issue about possibly installing a traffic light on Main Street, but the town council members have recently been changed. He needs to be able to look up the new council members and their contact information.
