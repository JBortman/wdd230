Purpose:

The general purpose of the site is for small businesses around the Pioche, NV area to gather and help build the local economy. This site will host announcements for events, local updates, weather, business spotlights, and contacts to reach out to members within the community throughout different pages for each topic. Not only building the economy, but also strengthening the community.
