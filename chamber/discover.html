Founded in the year 1869, Pioche became a booming mining town for silver. Now with a population of over 1,400 and growing, this town is anything BUT a ghost town.

Come and visit the "Million Dollar Courthouse" and learn about some of the history of the town, and how the law was handled in one of the wildest towns in the West!

There are great fishing opportunities in the nearby lakes, that also accomodate for camping! In the big Eagle Valley resevoir, you can also bring a small boat.

There are a couple state parks not too far from the town as well, including Cathedral Gorge (pictured above) and Oak Springs Trilobite Site.

Just beyond town limits, there are hundreds of miles of trails to explore on any OHV vehicle. From wide open graded roads to tight mountain trails and everything in between, perfect for any outdoor sport enthusiast!
